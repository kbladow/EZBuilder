EZBuilder =========

What is EZResume? Why should I use this?

When applying for a job, the employer often requests a copy of your resume. Employers will expect to see your job history, education and skills on a resume.

EZResume is an easy to use tool that will guide you through the process to create a complete resume. When using EZResume, you won’t need any special computer skills besides typing and using a mouse. It will do all of the work for you. All you have to do is type in the information it asks for. At the end, you will have a clean looking resume that you can start using right away to start applying for jobs!

What information do I need to know before I start?

Before you begin using EZResume, it will be extremely helpful to have the following information. Having this information before you start will save you time while answering the questions. If you sign up to create an account before or after you use EZResume, you will then be able to save your information and come back to it later.

When you open the EZResume tool, you will be asked questions that can be answered with the information below. Remember, collecting and organizing this information before you start will save you time.

Personal Information
  • It is very important for employers to have a way to contact you, including email, phone (cell phone or home phone). If you need to create an email account there are many options. Here are a few:
    • Gmail
    • Yahoo
  • To learn more about how you can use email, visit Goodwill Community Foundation’s email tutorial.

Work History

Employers will want the following information about each of the jobs that you have had.

  • Job Title
  • Name of company
  • Month and year that you started and ended at each job.
  • Job responsibilities
  • For examples of responsibilities for a specific job, visit the Occupational Outlook Handbook(http://www.bls.gov/ooh/).
Education history

Employers will need to know that you have the necessary education for the job. The guide will ask about your education history. This includes GED or high school diploma, college degree and/or certifications. Be sure to have school names, location (city and state) and the dates you attended. Even if you did not complete a program, it is important to include it on your resume.

Skills

It is very important to highlight any skills that you have. Think about what you’re good at, especially if it is relevant to the job that you are applying for. The guide will provide you a list of possible skills but you’ll also be given the option to include your own.

How can I edit my resume?

You will be able to move between sections of EZResume if you want to make changes to your information. Once you answered all the questions, you will also be given the option to make changes or save your answers by signing up for an account.

When you click on sign up to save your work, you will be able to easily return and make changes. If you choose not to sign up, you have to ability to download your newly created resume which you can then save on your computer or flash drive.
